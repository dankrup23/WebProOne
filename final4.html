Movement

During some classroom activities, students will be required to move. This can occur, for example, while doing
group assignments or a lab activities. However, even during independent work, where students are mainly in their seats,
some movement may still be required. For these reasons, it is necessary for them to understand when and what kind of
movement is permitted, as well as how it is to be done.




Types of Movement

Used during teacher-led instruction

Used during independent work and tests/qizzes

Used during bell ringer and some group activities

Used during most lab and group activities
