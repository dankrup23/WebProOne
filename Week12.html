In any K-12 classroom, beginning teachers, as well as those more experienced, may find that their students are not performing at the academic levels that are well within their capabilities. There is a classroom management strategy that is taught to educators in many school districts, and can be developed by any teacher, in any classroom. This is known as CHAMPS, which stands for Conversation, Help, Activity, Movement, Participation, and Success.
