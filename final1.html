Conversation

Controlling the conversation level in your classroom is essential in keeping your it well-ordered and maintained.
In fact, most classroom management issues occur because students are talking. However, it is not the fact that
they are talking, but what they are talking about that needs to be managed. As you will see in your classroom,
students often learn just as well from each other as they do the teacher. You can take advantage of this to increase
your student success.





Conversation Levels

No Talking

Students do not talk on this level
Usually posted during independent work, such as taking a test

Whisper Voice

Students keep voice level to a whisper
Usually posted partner work

Normal Voice

Students keep voice level to a normal conversation
Usually posted group work

Outside Voice

Students talk they way they would outside
Usually posted during a class game or competition
