Welcome Message

In any K-12 classroom, beginning teachers, as well as those more experienced, may find that their students are not performing
at the academic levels that are well within their capabilities. While there are many factors that determine an individual studentâ€™s
performance, in addition to ways that a teacher can assist that student, one feature that every classroom must have is a well-managed
learning environment. There is a classroom management strategy that is taught to educators in many school districts, and can be developed
by any teacher, in any classroom. This is known as CHAMPS, which stands for: Conversation, Help, Activity, Movement, Participation,
and Success.
