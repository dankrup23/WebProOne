Success

After the students complete a classroom activity, it is important to let them know that they did a good job.
The best way to do this, of course, is by having them receive praise. There are many ways that this can be done,
and it is up to the teacher to decide which is best for his or her class. In using all of the CHAMPS procedures,
you can accomplish the ultimate goal in any classroom, which is STUDENT SUCCESS!
