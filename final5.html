Participation

In a classroom and throughout the class period, learning activities will be taking place.
On most days, these activities will change throughout the class period. The length of a specific
acitivity may last from a few minutes, to most of the class period. Therefore, it is essential
that the students understand how they are to participate at any given time. As with all procedures,
this should be posted in the classroom for all to see.





Types of Participation

No talking
Students are actively listening to the teacher
Students raise hand when they have a question

Students discuss activity with their partner
Teacher must make sure that all conversations relate to the activity

Students discuss activity with their partners in the group
As with partner work, teacher must make sure that all conversations relate to the activity

Students complete activity on their own
No talking
Used during Independent Work and Tests/Quizzes
